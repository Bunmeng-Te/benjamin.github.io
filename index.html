Achievements

Since the very beginning of my academic life, I studied at Beltei International School, I dedicated myself to my studies and considered it as my second home. By the end of 2022, I attended Cambodian Grade 12 National Exam and achieved a grade A with an impressive overall band score of 99.525.

During grade 12, I also took part in the prestigious Cambodia Outstanding Student Exam in mathematics, I secured the remarkable 4th rank, further reinforcing my commitment to the world of pure mathematics and honing my critical skills in both algebra and geometry problem-solving.

One significant aspect of my educational journey that has always held a special place in my heart is my profound love for STEM, mathematics. From a very young age, I found myself drawn to the beauty and complexity of numbers and equations. Solving numerous mathematics problems has become a cherished habit of mine. During my time in grade 9, I also had the opportunity to truly showcase my mathematical abilities when I participated in the Cambodia Outstanding Student Exam, organized by the Ministry of Education, Youth, and Sports. I was elated to receive a gold medal for my performance among the 80 competitors from all the provinces and cities in Cambodia.

Beyond national competitions, I sought to challenge myself on an international level. Even though it was during the pandemic, I was trying my best to actively participate in renowned competitions such as the Singapore and Asian Schools Math Olympiad (SASMO) and the Asia International Mathematical Olympiad (AIMO). My dedication and hard work paid off as I was awarded a gold and a silver medal, respectively, in these prestigious events.
